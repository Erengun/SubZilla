# SubZilla

**register:** product

## Product Name

SubZilla

## Product Purpose

A personal subscription tracker that helps individual consumers monitor recurring charges, track renewal dates, and maintain budget awareness for all active subscriptions in one place. Data stays private on the device.

## Users

Individual consumers managing multiple subscriptions (streaming services, software, memberships, etc.) who want visibility into what they're paying and when.

## Brand Tone

Clean, minimal, native-feeling. iOS-first aesthetic. Functional over decorative. Calm, uncluttered, approachable. Modern without being trendy.

## Anti-References

Cluttered finance dashboards. Neon SaaS UIs. Aggressive gradient backgrounds. Skeuomorphic designs. Feature bloat.

## Color Strategy

Coral (#D83434) used sparingly as accent for key actions and highlights. Supports light and dark modes via Material 3. Primary palette: neutral grays, with coral drawing attention to subscriptions due soon or expiring.
