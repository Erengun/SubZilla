# Privacy Policy for SubZilla

Effective date: March 15, 2026
Last updated: March 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SubZilla ("the App") processes information when you use the app.

SubZilla is designed as a local-first subscription tracker. Most data is stored only on your device.

## 1) Data Controller and Contact

Data controller:
- Legal entity/name: **DevOpen**
- Contact email: **info@devopen.io**
- Mailing address: **Not publicly listed**

If you have privacy questions or requests, contact us at the email above.

## 2) Information We Process

### A. Information you provide in the app
Stored locally on your device:
- Profile information (for example name, email, profile image)
- Subscription entries (for example subscription name, amount, billing frequency, dates, labels/colors)
- App settings (for example language, currency, theme, onboarding completion status)

### B. Device and app functionality data
Used for app features:
- Local notification scheduling data (to remind you about due subscriptions)
- Exported backup files you choose to create (JSON)

### C. Optional third-party transfer (Gravatar)
If you choose to use the **Connect Gravatar** feature, the app sends a hash of your email address to Gravatar to retrieve your avatar image. This transfer is optional and user-initiated.

### D. What we do not currently do
Based on the current app implementation, we do **not** run our own backend account service and we do **not** include analytics SDKs or crash-reporting SDKs in the app at this time.

## 3) How We Use Information

We process information to:
- Operate core app features (subscription tracking, analytics screens, calendar views)
- Save your preferences and profile settings
- Create and import/export local backups on your request
- Send local reminder notifications
- Retrieve an avatar from Gravatar only when you request it

## 4) Legal Bases (GDPR)

Where GDPR applies, legal bases may include:
- **Performance of a contract**: providing app features you request
- **Legitimate interests**: maintaining app functionality and reliability
- **Consent**: when you choose optional features (for example Gravatar)
- **Legal obligations**: if required by applicable law

## 5) Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information.

We do not share your in-app subscription database with our own servers because the app is local-first.

Limited sharing can occur when:
- You use optional third-party features (for example Gravatar)
- You explicitly export/share files from your device
- Distribution platforms (for example Apple App Store / Google Play) process app distribution and related operational data under their own policies

## 6) International Data Transfers

If you use optional third-party services (such as Gravatar), data may be processed outside your country, including in jurisdictions that may have different data protection rules.

## 7) Data Retention

Most app data is retained locally on your device until you:
- Edit or delete the data inside the app
- Remove exported backup files
- Uninstall the app (which generally removes app-local data, subject to device/OS behavior)

We do not currently maintain a server-side account database for your subscription content.

## 8) Security

We use reasonable measures appropriate to a local-first mobile app. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure. You are responsible for securing your device and any exported backup files.

## 9) Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including access, correction, deletion, portability, objection/restriction, and withdrawal of consent.

To exercise rights, contact us at: **info@devopen.io**

Because most app data is stored locally, many actions (view, edit, delete) can be performed directly in the app/device.
